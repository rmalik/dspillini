Do you have a scholarship?

We offer three (3) $500 scholarships to pledges and two (2) $500 scholarships to active members each semester. The application and additional information can be found on our scholarship page.

When should I make my payments?

For Pledges, the initial pledging fee is due at the start of pledge education. The initiation fee must be paid by the day of the initiation ceremony (end of the semester).

For Active Members, all payments are due at the beginning of the semester.

Are there payment plans available?

In the past we have tried to be flexible to accommodate those with financial difficulties. The eligibility of a payment plan is on a case-by-case basis and will be decided by the Treasurer.

What are my payment method options?

All payments should be preferably in a check made out to 'Delta Sigma Phi Fraternity'. Cash will also be accepted. Credit Cards and/or PayPal are not accepted at this time.

Who should I pay?

All payments should be given directly to the Treasurer. In the event that he is unavailable it is recommended to leave your check in the mailbox of the Treasurer (located in the mailroom of the house)

How do I get a single or double room?

Room selection is based on a few factors: availability, seniority and level of participation in the fraternity. A single room is not guaranteed.

Have more questions?

If you have any questions regarding financial issues, feel free to email treasurer AT illinideltasigs.org
